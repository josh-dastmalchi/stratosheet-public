Stratosheet

Stratosheet aims to bring users the familiarity of working with spreadsheets coupled with improved data quality and the power of integrating with online services.

Validation

Stratosheet sees your data as more than just text - it can represent numbers, dates, other users, and more. This allows you to fine-tune your data and validation.

Spreadsheets offer little in terms of data validation. Simple data entry errors can lead to lost time and revenue. Ensure that your data is correct, complete, and consistent.

Integrations

Tired of manually sending order updates every time you update your data? Wish you could be notified when someone changes something? Stratosheet can help with that.

We offer a growing number of chat and email integrations to keep you informed.

Customization

Stratosheet is designed to be flexible. You define everything about your data and how you view it.

Hierarchies

Spreadsheets are flat. The real world isn't.

Stratosheet allows you to reference data from one table from another. For example, your orders can reference your customers, and your customers can reference your salespeople.
